Create the following folders in the following locations:
- 'Sounds' in Lumberyard\1.1.0.0\dev\GameSDK
	- drop 'wwise' & 'wwise_project' into 'Sounds'
- Go to Lumberyard\1.1.0.0\dev\GameSDK\libs\GameAudio
	- drop 'wwise' & 'wwise_project' here

In Lumberyard Editor:
1. Open Audio Controls Editor or 'Headphones' icon
2. Create a new folder for your sounds in the left-hand menu.
3. Audio should appear in WWise Controls or 'right-hand menu'. Drag it into your left-hand folder (should appear as a 'Trigger'). 
   Right-click and 'Execute Trigger' to play.
